Requesting for Transfers
  1. Select a location you want to be transferred to.
  2. If you have atleast 2 years on duty and the port of request is available, you will be allowed to transfer.
  3. Handlers are not allowed to be deployed in their city of origin.
  4. Click on confirm button to complete transfer, you will be notified of your date of transfer.
